Questions

Everything you might want to ask before buying.

What does Distlo actually deliver for one Starter Pack? +

You send one raw input — a voice note, a recording, a transcript, or a paragraph of thoughts. Within 3 working days you receive: 5 LinkedIn posts written in your voice, 10 opening hooks you can use or adapt, 3 call-to-action options, and one carousel concept with a content brief. Everything is ready to post. You do not write a single word.

Why is everything async? Why no calls? +

Because calls are where good intentions go to die. A 30-minute briefing call costs you an hour of your day with travel and context-switching. A voice note costs you five minutes — and we get more from it because you're not performing for a camera.

Distlo is built around the way busy professionals actually work: send when you have a thought, receive when you can review.

How is this different from hiring a social media manager? +

Distlo is not a replacement for a social media manager. A social media manager handles content creation, posting, engagement, analytics, paid campaigns, and multiple platforms.

Distlo does one specific thing: it turns the thinking you already do into LinkedIn-ready content. That's it. It is a specialist tool for one specific problem — you have valuable thinking that never makes it to LinkedIn because you don't have time to write.

How is this different from using ChatGPT myself? +

Three things AI on its own doesn't give you. First: the SIGNAL Framework — a structured method for finding what's actually worth saying in your raw input, not just summarising it. Second: niche calibration — every pack is shaped against the conversation already happening in your niche so your voice fills a gap, not echoes the script. Third: human review — every deliverable goes through a quality check before it reaches you.

If a generic AI prompt produced LinkedIn-ready content, every founder using ChatGPT would have a strong feed by now. Most don't.

Will the posts sound like me — or like AI? +

Like you. SIGNAL builds from your raw input, not from a template. Your tone, your phrasing patterns, your way of thinking — those stay intact. We are amplifying what you already do, not replacing your voice with a house style.

If you write long and reflective, your posts will be long and reflective. If you write short and punchy, that's what you get back.

What kind of input works best? +

Anything where your real thinking comes through. A 5–10 minute voice note recorded between meetings is often perfect. A recorded webinar, a workshop session, a rough paragraph you typed at midnight — all work.

The minimum standard: 5 minutes of substantive spoken content, or 500 words of genuine written thinking. The format matters less than the substance.

What does the revision round cover? +

Each pack includes one revision round. A valid revision covers adjustments to up to 3 posts, up to 5 hooks, CTA focus changes, and one revision to the carousel concept — all using the same original raw input.

Revision requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery. Additional revisions beyond the included one are charged at ₹1,499 per round.

What's the turnaround time really? +

3 working days from the moment we accept your brief. Working days are Monday to Friday, IST, excluding Indian public holidays.

If you submit a brief on Friday evening and we accept it Monday morning, the timer starts Monday. If anything changes, you'll know within 24 hours.

Why is payment 100% upfront? +

Because Distlo runs on capacity, not on staff. When we accept your brief, your slot is reserved and the work begins. Holding back payment means holding back the work.

This is standard for specialist services delivered by individual operators. The signed agreement protects both sides.

Can I cancel after I've paid? +

If you cancel before submitting a brief, or before we have accepted it, no cash refund is issued — instead, we issue a 60-day credit equal to the full order value, valid for one future submission. Once we've accepted your brief and content generation has started, the payment is non-refundable.

The full cancellation terms are in the signed client agreement.

Do you work with international clients? +

Yes. Distlo is currently delivering to clients in India and the UAE, and accepting orders from Singapore, the UK, and other markets. International payments are handled through Razorpay and Cashfree depending on the pack and currency.

The service is fully async — your time zone makes no difference to delivery.

Who actually owns the content I receive? +

You do. All delivered content becomes your sole property after full payment. Distlo retains no rights or licence over your output once it's delivered.

The SIGNAL Framework itself remains proprietary to Distlo — only the output generated by applying it to your input is transferred to you.

What is the retainer actually for? +

Exit from the content treadmill. Your LinkedIn never goes dark. You send your thinking when you have it; we turn each one around in 3 working days. Your slot is reserved. Your voice file is maintained — every month the output gets sharper because we know how you think.

Up to 4 inputs per month. One active batch at a time. Unused inputs do not roll over. Month-to-month, cancel any time.

Why should I choose the retainer over individual packs? +

Starter packs are for one-off bursts. The retainer locks in capacity every month — your slot is held whether you use it or not. You're not comparing the retainer to a few individual packs; you're comparing it to the alternative of doing it yourself every month or hiring someone who needs briefings, calls, and management.

None of that is required here.

How do I know the quality is real? +

Read the sample packs. Each one is a full demonstration of exactly what you'd receive — same format, same depth, same SIGNAL Framework. If the posts, hooks, and carousel concept in the relevant pack are stronger than what you're currently producing, that's your answer.

If they aren't, the service isn't for you yet — and that's also useful to know.

What happens to my data after delivery? +

Raw input files are retained for 30 days after delivery and then permanently deleted. Generated outputs stay accessible for 180 days. Signed agreements and tax-relevant records are kept for the periods required by Indian law.

Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
