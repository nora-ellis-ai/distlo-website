Summary of how Distlo works

This summary is not a substitute for the signed client agreement. It exists to set expectations clearly before any engagement.

  • Scope. Each pack delivers 5 LinkedIn posts, 10 hooks, 3 CTAs, and 1 Canva carousel concept, generated using the SIGNAL Framework from one raw input you submit.
  • Payment. 100% upfront. Work begins after payment is confirmed and the brief is accepted in writing.
  • Delivery. 3 working days from brief acceptance. Working days are Monday to Friday, IST, excluding Indian public holidays.
  • Revisions. One revision round included per pack. Revision requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Cancellation. No cash refund after payment. A 60-day credit equal to the order value is issued under defined cancellation scenarios.
  • Async only. The service is fully asynchronous. No calls, no meetings, no live sessions are offered.
  • Ownership. All delivered content becomes your property after full payment. The SIGNAL Framework itself remains proprietary to Distlo.
  • Liability. Distlo's maximum liability for any claim is capped at the amount paid for the specific order in dispute.
  • Governing law. Indian law. Disputes are resolved through good-faith negotiation followed by arbitration in Mumbai if required.
